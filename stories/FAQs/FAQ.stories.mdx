## 1.  Image Wrapper vs. Video Wrapper

In neeto-editor, the `neeto-editor__image-wrapper` and `neeto-editor__video-wrapper` class names serve distinct
purposes:

- **Image Wrapper (`neeto-editor__image-wrapper`):**
The `neeto-editor__image-wrapper` class is assigned to the `div` element wrapping the uploaded images and videos. It adds styles to images and videos.
- **Video Wrapper (`neeto-editor__video-wrapper`):**
  The `neeto-editor__video-wrapper` class is assigned to the `div` element wrapping `iframes`. It adds styles to embed elements.
